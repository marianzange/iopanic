Hello, I'm Marian.

Past companies I’ve co-founded include ezeep, a cloud printing service for education and enterprises (acquired by Cortado AG) and Liquid Labs, a formerly Cambridge, MA based startup focused on natural language processing - primarily text summarization and clustering. I’ve also been involved in a variety of other projects in both, software engineering and consulting roles.
